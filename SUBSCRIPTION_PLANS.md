# Subscription Plans for School Cloud SaaS

Our platform offers flexible subscription plans designed to meet the needs of schools of all sizes. Choose the plan that best suits your institution's requirements:

## 1. Starter Lite Plan
- **Pricing:** $2 per student per month
- **Features:** Ideal for small schools or those just starting with our platform.
- **Scalability:** Pay only for the number of students enrolled, making it a cost-effective solution as your school grows.

## 2. Business Plan
- **Pricing:** $100 per month
- **User Capacity:** Supports up to 2,000 users.
- **Features:** Comprehensive set of tools for managing school operations, suitable for medium-sized institutions.
- **Cost Efficiency:** Fixed monthly fee provides predictable budgeting for schools with larger student bodies.

## 3. Enterprise Plan
- **Pricing:** $150 per month
- **User Capacity:** Supports between 2,000 and 4,000 users.
- **Features:** Tailored solutions for larger educational institutions that require advanced functionalities and scalability.
- **Flexible Support:** Enjoy dedicated support and additional customization options to fit your unique institutional needs.

## 4. Premium Plan
- **Pricing:** $300 per month
- **User Capacity:** Supports between 4,000 and 10,000 users.
- **Features:** Designed for large educational institutions needing extensive user support and advanced features.
- **Enhanced Customization:** Access premium support and additional functionalities to cater to high-demand environments.

---

Each plan is designed to accommodate the varying needs and sizes of educational institutions, ensuring that you only pay for what you need as your school evolves. Choose the plan that aligns best with your goals and join us in improving educational management!
